A Higher Standard

​

Carpentry is second nature to me. I learned hands-on from an early age, refining my craft over the years into a focused, efficient approach. It’s not just about building—it’s about understanding how materials, design, and structure come together.

 

Working in the Hamptons, I’ve developed a style that fits the expectations of this place—where precision, execution, and attention to detail matter. Every project is different, but the process stays the same: clean, seamless, and built with intention.
